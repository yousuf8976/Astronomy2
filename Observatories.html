TOP OBSERVATORIES AROUND THE WORLD

Journey Through Space with World-Class Observatories

Discover some of the best observatories in the world. These state-of-the-art facilities provide breathtaking views of the cosmos, and their locations are just as impressive!


Mauna Kea Observatories

Mauna Kea Observatories is located atop Mauna Kea, a dormant volcano in Hawaii.

Paranal Observatory

Located in the Atacama Desert in Chile, Paranal is one of the most advanced observatories on Earth.

Palomar Observatory

Located in California, USA, Palomar is known for housing the Hale Telescope, once the largest in the world.

Royal Greenwich Observatory

Located in the UK, the Royal Observatory in Greenwich is famous for its historical importance in timekeeping and astronomy.
